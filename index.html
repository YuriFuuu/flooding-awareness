Financial Losses by States

Based on data from fiscal year 2024, despite flooding impacting the entire nation, we focus on the ten states with the highest financial losses following these disasters. The bar chart below highlights the top 10 states with the most significant financial impacts, showing the number of records for closed losses with payment, closed losses without payment, and open losses (active claims still unresolved). Texas and Florida lead the list in terms of claims, but notably, Iowa, with fewer than one-tenth the claims of these states, still ranks 10th in recovery costs.
